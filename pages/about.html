Who are these people??

Quarticore is a small team of developers (I'm sure you've heard that one before) with a common goal of making neat things. Throughout our younger years, many of us have strived to, in some way, contribute something of our own to the world of video games. With our collective experience in different mediums, such as sound or design, we made a well-rounded team that can, eventually, create something awesome.
