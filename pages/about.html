Who are these people??

Quarticore is a small team of developers (I'm sure you've heard that one before) with a common goal of making neat things. Throughout our younger years (some of us have known eachother as long ago as elementary school!), many of us have strived to, in some way, contribute something of our own to the world of video games. With our collective experience in different mediums, such as sound or design, we made a well-rounded team that can, eventually, create something awesome.

Open Source

We are all believers of open-source software. We will always strive to make everything that we can available for free to the public. Of course, our own full games, assets, and code are probably not on the table (unless they were released for free already), but any tooling we create will usually be made available for anyone to use.
